## Service Level Agreements (SLA)
We offer tiered response times based on incident severity:

| Severity Level | Response SLA (Bronze) | Response SLA (Enterprise Gold) |
|---|---|---|
| **L1 — System Outage** | 8 Hours | 1 Hour (24x7) |
| **L2 — Critical Vulnerability Scan Block** | 24 Hours | 4 Hours (Business Hours) |
| **L3 — General Usage & Inquiries** | 48 Hours | 12 Hours (Business Hours) |
